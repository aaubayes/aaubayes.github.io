About

A place where we learn statistics beyond formulas.

What we do

We present a research topic, paper, method, or ongoing idea, then open the room to discussion. The format is intentionally flexible: shared questions, thoughtful exchange, and space to think together.

Who shows up

Statistics PhDs, ML researchers from the technical sciences faculty, computational social scientists, and curious students from across campus. New attendees welcome at any session.
